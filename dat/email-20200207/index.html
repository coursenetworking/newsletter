Dear CN User,

IMPORTANT NOTE

With our recent move to a completely mobile responsive site, the native mobile app is no longer needed and will be removed from the iOS App Store and Google Play Store on March 2nd. Don’t worry, you can still access CN easily via the web browser on your mobile devices. Click here to learn how to add CN to the Home screen of your phone.

Here’s a few new things you can find on thecn.com now:

  • Record videos: you now have the ability to easily record videos straight from your desktop or mobile device
  • ePortfolio updates on Home Feed: Adding to your ePortfolio? Great! Now you can share it with your followers, classmates, and more on your Home Feed
  • Add personal reflections to your ePortfolio: Do some of your sections need more explanation? You can now add personal reflections to different sections of the ePortfolio such as badges and Showcases
  • Endorsements: Endorse others’ recommendations, expertise, or showcases on their ePortfolio to show your support and help validate their hard work

Coming soon:

  • Course Path tool for self-paced online learning
  • Recommendation invitation and more ePortfolio features and refinements
  • Badges – more badges will be added to the CN badge library for course instructors to use

Thanks,
The CN Team
